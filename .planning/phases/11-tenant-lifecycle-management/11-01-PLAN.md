     - If `!tenant || !tenant.isActive`, throw error: `'Tenant account is deactivated'`

  In `api/auth/auth.service.js` ‚Äî `refreshAccessToken()` function:

     - If deactivated, throw: `'Tenant account is deactivated - refresh denied'`

  2. Verify no changes to super-admin middleware or super-admin service
