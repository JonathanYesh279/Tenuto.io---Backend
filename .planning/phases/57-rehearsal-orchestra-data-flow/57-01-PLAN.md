<objective>
Refactor rehearsal create/delete operations to use MongoDB transactions for atomic orchestra.rehearsalIds synchronization.

Purpose: Currently, addRehearsal and removeRehearsal update the orchestra's rehearsalIds array in a separate non-transactional step that silently swallows failures. This creates orphan references (orchestra points to deleted rehearsals) or missing references (rehearsal exists but orchestra doesn't know about it). The fix wraps both operations in withTransaction so they succeed or fail together.

Output: rehearsal.service.js with transactional addRehearsal, removeRehearsal, and bulkCreateRehearsals functions.
</objective>
